Ingredients

  • 1 cup Flour
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Pour batter on a hot pan.
  3. Flip when bubbles appear.
  4. Serve with honey or syrup!
